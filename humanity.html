The World Painting was born in the United
Arab Emirates on October 28, 2021, at the
international EXPO in Dubai.

Unite people South America

Its idea was both simple and profound: to unite people from all over the world — regardless of their beliefs, culture, skin color, or origin.

Humanity Eurasia

In that moment, the world became one. People from different nations took up the brush and left their mark on a single canvas — in the name of humanity, peace, and the future.

United Nations South America

The World Painting brings together all United Nations member states, as well as a number of territories and regions with special status.

In total, the project has united more than 2,080 participants from over 200 countries and territories.

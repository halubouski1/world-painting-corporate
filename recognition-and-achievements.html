Global Recognition and Achievements

One brush. One world. One humanity.

People from around the world became part of a single movement, where each person passed the brush as a symbol of peace, connecting continents, cultures, and destinies.

Guinness World Record Holder

World Painting set a world record for the largest number of people in an online video chain passing a paintbrush from one to another.

Beyond Earth

At the Top of the World

The World Painting flag, created on canvas, was raised to the highest point on Earth - the summit of Mount Everest.

This ascent was accomplished by Maksut Zhumayev - captain of the Kazakhstan national mountaineering team, and a climber who has conquered all 14 eight-thousanders of the world.

The Coin of Peace

Ag 925 62,2 g (2 oz) Diameter - 50 mm Color printing technology

The National Bank of the Republic of Kazakhstan issued a commemorative coin dedicated to the World Painting project.

At its center is the dove of peace - an image imbued with profound meaning and history.

Ag 925 62,2 g (2 oz) Diameter - 50 mm Color printing technology

It was created by artist Karipbek Kuyukov - a man who has become a symbol of resilience and humanism.

Born without arms near the Semipalatinsk nuclear test site, he devoted his life to art and to the pursuit of peace, becoming a prominent voice of the anti-nuclear movement «ATOM».

Sport and Art for Peace

World Painting joined forces with the World Boxing Council WBC - one of the most influential sports organizations in the world.

This collaboration became a space where the power of sport and the language of art came together in a single message - a message of peace, humanity, and unity.

100 Knockout Night

As part of the anniversary event «100 Knockout Night» at the legendary MGM Grand Arena in Las Vegas, 100 exclusive medals were created - symbols not only of sporting achievement, but of humanity's global aspiration for peace.

These medals were awarded to outstanding athletes and renowned boxers as a recognition of strength directed toward creation.

World Painting Peace Belt

The idea continued with the World Painting Peace Belt - a special belt of peace created in collaboration with WBC.

Wladimir Klitschko

It was presented to the legendary Wladimir Klitschko by Lennox Lewis - as a symbol of strength, responsibility, and peace.

Terence Crawford

The belt was presented to Terence Crawford, symbolically bringing sport and art together into a shared space of values.
